Chromatin Immunoprecipitation (ChIP-qPCR)

Purpose: Study imprinting-associated chromatin marks.

Steps:

  1. Crosslink cells with formaldehyde
  2. Lyse nuclei and sonicate chromatin
  3. Immunoprecipitate using antibody
  4. Reverse crosslink and purify DNA
  5. Perform qPCR

Notes: Optimize sonication cycles carefully.
